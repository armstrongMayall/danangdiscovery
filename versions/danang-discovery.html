How to Play

  1. Get your Danang Discovery Card with 10 activities.
  2. Visit each attraction or cultural experience on your card.
  3. Take a selfie at each stop and post it on Instagram with our hashtag: #DanangDiscovery.
  4. Tick off each activity in the app as you go.
  5. Show your 10 Instagram posts to our reception team.
  6. The first guest to finish each day wins a complimentary dinner for two at our signature restaurant.
